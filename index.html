Welcome To Our Company

Shiva Structures Pvt. Ltd.

Shiva Structures Pvt. Ltd., founded in 1991, is a leading construction company in India, specializing in large-scale irrigation, infrastructure, and civil engineering projects. With expertise in sectors like dams, canals, pipelines, tunnels, and slope stabilization, the company has successfully completed over 100 projects across India.

Under the leadership of Shri. Madhukar Deshmukh and a skilled team, the firm is known for its commitment to quality, innovation, and timely project delivery. Shiva Structures also emphasizes sustainability, safety, and community welfare, offering job opportunities and social initiatives.

WHY CHOOSE US?

Shiva Structures Pvt. Ltd. is a trusted leader in large-scale infrastructure projects, known for its expertise, quality, and on-time delivery. With over 30 years of experience, the company consistently exceeds client expectations in safety and value.

OUR SERVICES

Irrigation Projects
Expertise in the construction and maintenance of irrigation systems, ensuring efficient water distribution for agricultural needs.
Dam & Barrage Construction
Skilled in designing and building large dams and barrages, supporting water management and flood control.
Canal Construction & Rehabilitation
Providing construction and restoration of canals for efficient water flow in irrigation and drinking water systems.
Pipelines & Aqueducts
Delivering robust pipeline and aqueduct solutions for the transport of water, gas, and other utilities.
Dam Rehabilitation
Specialized in rehabilitating and strengthening existing dams to improve their safety and functionality.
Slope Stabilization & Rockfall Protection
Implementing cutting-edge solutions for slope stabilization and rockfall protection to safeguard infrastructure and lives.
Drilling & Grouting
Offering advanced drilling and grouting services for ground stabilization and strengthening structures.
Tunnels Construction
Expert in the construction of tunnels for transportation, water supply, and other infrastructure projects.
Major Bridges & Infrastructure
Designing and building long-span bridges and complex infrastructure projects that ensure connectivity and durability.
Operations & Maintenance
Providing ongoing maintenance and operational services to ensure the long-term functionality of completed projects.
Concrete Works
Delivering high-quality concrete services for construction projects, including foundations, structures, and roadways.
Liaisoning Services
Offering efficient liaisoning services to navigate government processes and secure necessary approvals for projects.

Our Forte

At Shiva Structures Pvt. Ltd., every project is crafted with passion and dedication, ensuring the highest standards of quality and precision. Our portfolio showcases a legacy of successful, impactful infrastructure projects that reflect our commitment to excellence.
