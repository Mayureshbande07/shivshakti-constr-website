Welcome to Our Company

Shiva Structures Pvt. Ltd.

Shiva Structures Pvt. Ltd., founded in 1991, is a leading construction company in India, specializing in large-scale irrigation, infrastructure, and civil engineering projects. With expertise in sectors like dams, canals, pipelines, tunnels, and slope stabilization, the company has successfully completed over 100 projects across India.

Under the leadership of Shri. Madhukar Deshmukh and a skilled team, the firm is known for its commitment to quality, innovation, and timely project delivery. Shiva Structures also emphasizes sustainability, safety, and community welfare, offering job opportunities and social initiatives.

Introduction

M/s. SHIVA STRUCTURES PRIVATE LIMITED is Aurangabad based Company engaged in the activity of Civil Works especially of Irrigation Projects, Dam, Barrages, Dam Rehabilitation / Repairs of Dams, Drilling & Grouting, Canals, M.I. Tank, K.T. Weir, Aqueducts, Cement Naala Bandhara, Site Excavation, Site Development, Pipeline Project, Roads, Bridges, Building Constructions, Industrial Sheds, Tunnel, Barrages, roads, rockfall protection, landslide and other allied works.

The Company was established in Year 1996 as a firm under the name of “Shiva Construction” and converted in Private Limited company in Oct 2008 as “Shiva Structures Pvt. Ltd.” The firm is consisting Two Partners who further continued as Directors of the Company and continued the Business of Contractors. It will thus be revealed the fact that the Company has got an Extensive and Exceptional Existence in the market and throughout its working has created its own Repute in the Irrigation Department of Government of Maharashtra.
